Hello Sultana





I’ve longed to write to you in a while, letting you know how I feel inside. You gave me everything to make my life glow, and I can’t thank you enough for being the sweetest thing in the world. How can I describe my feelings for you in this short letter? This space won’t be enough to contain everything I feel about you. You keep showing me that in every age and any place, true love exists, and I’m so happy to have found you when I did. Having you in my world has brought me more satisfaction than anything can bring, including money. I can’t thank you enough for touching me deeply with the things you do for me. You are the best thing that came my way, and I feel alive each time I get to see your lovely face again. Have a beautiful day, baby.



My love, I wish I am there with you because it’s lonely being here without you. I’ve tried to do so many things, but it keeps hitting me deep in my heart that I am missing someone so precious.

I’ll show you how much I admire you. I’ll give you my whole love because it was prepared specially for you. I can’t wait to be in your arms and kiss your soft lips when my head can’t think of anything to
I want to be the best for you so we will have a bright and fantastic future together, where the world will watch our love in wonder. I love you with all that I am, all that I was and all that I will ever be.
